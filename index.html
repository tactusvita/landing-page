ML-POWERED MODELS

Our models are fine-tuned for cellular insights and optimized therapy selection

Genomic Mapping

Our model UNA serves as a navigator, delving into the intricate landscapes within a patient's cells. It maps gene interactions, regulatory regions, and cellular behaviors to uncover patterns that define health and disease. By creating this comprehensive map, we can identify the most promising targets for therapy, laying the foundation for precision medicine.

Therapy Ranking

In contrast, TSAR is the strategist, piecing together the complex puzzle of a patient's condition. It evaluates and ranks therapies by analyzing the connections between treatments, genes, and the patient's biological profile. This way, we ensure the selection of optimal treatments, balancing effectiveness with minimal side effects for the best possible outcomes.

SCIENCE & TECHNOLOGY

We integrate multi-omic information into the decision layers of our predictive models

Single-Cell Analysis

TactusVita utilizes advanced single-cell analysis to examine cellular dynamics at an unprecedented resolution. By investigating gene interactions, regulatory pathways, and cellular behavior, the platform identifies key patterns that influence health and disease progression. These insights enable the discovery of precise therapy targets, driving breakthroughs in precision medicine and innovative research.

Heterogeneous Graph Networks

TactusVita employs heterogeneous graph networks to map relationships between therapies, genes, and patient-specific factors. UNA uses these networks to uncover molecular and genomic interactions, while TSAR ranks therapies based on efficacy and compatibility. Together, they enable clinicians and researchers to navigate and analyze complex datasets for more effective decision-making.

Geometric Deep Learning

Geometric deep learning powers TactusVita's ability to analyze multi-dimensional data, from tumor progression to cellular pathway dynamics. UNA leverages this technology to identify intricate gene interactions, while TSAR applies it to predict therapy success and toxicity risks. This ensures the platform delivers highly accurate insights for both patient care and clinical trial optimization.

Advanced Genomics

TactusVita integrates advanced genomics by processing in vitro, ex vivo, and in vivo data, including genetic tests, organoid sensitivity assays, and imaging results. UNA analyzes genomic pathways to pinpoint actionable targets, while TSAR uses these findings to rank therapies and refine treatment strategies. This combination accelerates discoveries in precision medicine and enhances outcomes for patients and clinical trials.
